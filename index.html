Texas

Welcome to Texas, the Lone Star State! Texas was the state of the union, joining it in 1845. It is the 2nd largest state in the United States, with over 30 million people, and also, the 2nd most populated state in the U.S. Three of its major cities, San Antonio, Houston, and its capital, Dallas, rank among the top 10 most populous cities in the country.
